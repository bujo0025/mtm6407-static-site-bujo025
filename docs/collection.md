# My UCS Collection

I have lots of Star Wars lego, but currently I only have two UCS sets. I have the Millennium Falcon and the Star Destroyer. Funny story about the Millennium Falcon actually. It was initally my older brothers, but over time his cat kept jumping on it and it slowly got destroyed. Eventually one day I decided to take on the challenege of rebuilding it for him. How hard could it be right? Well he gave it to me, in a million pieces, in a black garbage bag of all things. So instead of having the luxury of going bag by bag like he did, I instead had to dump the entire 7,500 piece set onto my bedroom floor and dig through it while building. In case you're not aware, there are many tiny little pieces that are on certain lego sets, pieces that are so small they can fit on the tip of your finger. You can imagine how annoying it was to dig through this pile of pieces to find tiny pieces like that. It was a painful few months for sure, but in the end it was rewarding to have rebuilt it.
